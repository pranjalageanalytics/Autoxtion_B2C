Payment

What is Stripe?

We are using Stripe is a third-party payment solution, that makes it possible to accept credit card and debit card payments. It's secure and quick - you can almost process payments immediately. Learn more about Stripe at www.stripe.com

Is Stripe secure?

Yes. Stripe follows the most strict requirements for security in the industry. Learn more about Stripe's security-https://stripe.com/docs/security

Is Stripe PCI compliant?

Yes. Stripe is certified as a PCI Level 1 Service Provider. -https://stripe.com/docs/security

How does Stripe handle false cards and orders?

Stripe prevents fraud by detecting activities that are suspicious. For example, transactions for repeated orders will be rejected. Stripe also works with global partners and credit card networks to monitor fraud. Learn more about how Stripe prevents fraud - https://support.stripe.com/questions/avoiding-fraud-and-disputes
